# Privacy Policy

WearBrowser is designed as a local-first browser.

## Data stored locally

The app may store the following on the device:

- Browsing history
- Bookmarks
- Open tabs
- Per-site settings such as zoom, user agent mode, reader mode, OLED mode and safe-area mode
- WebView cache, cookies and site storage managed by Android WebView

## Data not collected by the project

WearBrowser does not include analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs or remote logging in the open-source baseline.

## Network access

Web pages you open are loaded through Android WebView. Those websites may receive normal browser requests and may set cookies according to WebView behavior.

## Clearing data

The app includes controls to clear local history and WebView cache. Android system settings can also be used to clear app storage completely.
